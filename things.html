EXPLORE THE EXCITEMENT AND ENTERTAINMENT OF LOS ANGELES

THINGS TO DO



Los Angeles is alive with non-stop excitement, culture, diversity, art, and of course, entertainment. The Raichand Inn at California Plaza puts you near everything−including the STAPLES Center, the L.A. Live Complex and the Los Angeles Convention Center. From museums and theme parks to the rich and famous, L.A. has a culture all its own. Cheer on one of several professional sports teams or tour Universal Studios HollywoodSM to get a behind-the-scenes look at how movies are made.

Discover the charm of downtown neighborhoods such as Chinatown, Little Tokyo, Olvera Street and Koreatown. Rub elbows with the stars on Rodeo Drive, go celebrity watching at a trendy Hollywood restaurant or compare your handprints to the movie stars outside Grauman′s Chinese Theatre. With so much to choose from, the City of Angels is a can′t−miss destination− and The Raichand Inn at California Plaza is your perfect L.A. home.
